page layout using flex-flow: column and flex shorthand

<section style="display:flex; flex-flow: column;">
    <box>Heading Line One<br />Heading Line Two</box>
    <div style="display:flex;">
        <box style="flex:0 0 100px;">This<br />does<br />not<br />grow<br />or<br />shrink</box>
        <box style="flex:1 2 auto;">Middle box: This grows and shrinks but shrinks at twice the rate of the right box</box>
        <box style="flex:0 1 200px;">Right box: Starts off at 200px and will not grow. It does shrink. When it shrinks, it shrinks half as fast as the middle box.</box>
    </div>
    <box>Footer</box>
</section>
